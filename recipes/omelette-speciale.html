Omelette Spéciale

Ingredients:

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced ham
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup fries

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced ham, onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, and frozen fries to the skillet. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and the fries are cooked through.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the cooked ingredients in the skillet.
  5. Sprinkle the grated cheese and chopped parsley over the mixture.
  6. Cook the omelette until the edges are set and the cheese is melted.
  7. Using a spatula, fold the omelette in half and continue cooking until the omelette is fully cooked.
  8. Serve the omelette with fries hot.
