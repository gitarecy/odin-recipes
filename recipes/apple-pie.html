Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pie crusts
  • 6 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, salt, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  4. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust.
  5. Dot the top of the filling with small pieces of butter.
  6. Cover the pie with the second pie crust. Seal the edges and cut several slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool before serving.
