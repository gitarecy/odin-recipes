Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen berries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
